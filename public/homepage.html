About the project
This project began during my early college days. Inspired by the immersive universe of Sword Art Online, I initially experimented with console logs and inputs in IntelliJ to create a simple introduction to the game. Although I set the idea aside for a few years, my love for this enchanting realm never waned. The journey behind this project is one of growth and rediscovery. I am committed to realizing the dream of creating a game that captures the essence of a fantasy world.
Future Goals: The development of Aloy is an ongoing journey. Future updates will include:
Player Interaction: Form guilds, participate in events, and build a community within the game.
Questing and Storytelling: Embark on epic quests that unravel the rich lore of Alfheim, with intricate storylines and memorable characters.
